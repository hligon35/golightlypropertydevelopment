01 Who we are

Built from the ground up

Thoughtful site work.
Lasting results.

Golightly Property Development helps property owners move confidently from untouched land to a finished, functional site. We bring hands-on attention to every phase—preparing the ground, solving drainage and access needs, and building a strong foundation for the next step.

Based in West Paducah, we serve projects across the surrounding Kentucky region with a straightforward approach: understand the land, plan the work and leave every property better prepared for what comes next.
